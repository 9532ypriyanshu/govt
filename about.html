ABOUT US :)

The institute was established in the year 1958. Initially it was started as Government Technical Institute. All India Council for Technical Educational renamed it as Government Polytechnic Kanpur to bring the uniformity in the pattern of technical education throughout the country in the year 1964. Government Polytechnic Kanpur Nagar is approved by AICTE Govt. of India and affiliated to Board of Technical Education UP (BTEUP) . ...The College has fully furnished air conditioned Computer Lab equipped with Broadband Internet facility and video projection facility. The institution is running 18 courses in CIVIL ENGINEERING , ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING , ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING , INSTRUMENTATION & CONTROL , MECHANICAL ENGINEERING (AUTOMOBILE) , MECHANICAL ENGINEERING (PRODUCTION) , TEXTILE CHEMISTRY , TEXTILE TECHNOLOGY , CHEMICAL ENGINEERING , INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY , DIPLOMA IN PAINT TECHNOLOGY , DIPLOMA IN PLASTIC MOULD TECHNOLOGY , P.G.DIPLOMA IN MARKETING AND SALES MANAGEMENT , P.G.DIPLOMA IN RETAIL MANAGEMENT , P.G.DIPLOMA IN ACCOUNTACY(WITH COMPUTERISED ACCOUNT AND TAXATION) , P.G. DIPLOMA IN WEB DESIGNING , P.G. DIPLOMA IN COMPUTER HARDWARE & NETWORKING , P.G. DIPLOMA IN COMPUTER APPLICATION with intake of 60 students in each. College has one unit of N.S.s.of 10 student of each branch , who are taking activity part for making awareness about the social obligation among youth. one day and special 10 day camp have been arranged villages, in which each day was dedicated for special cause, which includes awareness about General health, AIDS awareness camps , Pulse Polio Campaign, Family planning program, Literacy campaign, Removal of social evils and pollution control awareness, Plantation and new devlopment in adriculture .
